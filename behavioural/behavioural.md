## Behavioural
Beharioural interviews are not something to overlook. Take it with same importance as other technical rounds.

1. Take a glance on [Amazon Leadership Principles](https://www.amazon.jobs/content/en/our-workplace/leadership-principles), probably 16 principles now. For each principle, prepare at least two stories from your own.
2. Read the pdf in this folder, or make a list of some commonly askes behavioural questions. Then try to frame your stories as part of practice.
3. Always try to answer the behavioural questions in [STAR method](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d2AnPgnCNZM)
